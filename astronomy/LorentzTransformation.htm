Introduction.

 

In this article the Lorentz transformations, providing the important formulae for the Relativity Theory, are derived.

For this purpose two coordinate systems are considered. One is in rest while the other is moving with a velocity v m/sec to the right. The most important assumption here is that the velocity of light c is the same in both coordinate systems
