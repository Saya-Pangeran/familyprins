WELCOME TO THE GENEALOGY PAGE OF THE PRINS FAMILY
 

 

From 2004 onwards we have been rather active in gathering information about our ancestors.
That is, the family branches Prins , Baas, Bisschop and Mollema and all, by marriage, related branches.
The families are mainly located in the provinces Zuid-Holland and Groningen in The Netherlands.
Since there is a continuous flow of new information available, this site will be frequently updated.
So we invite you to visit this site regularly to browse through the updated and more accurate information.
